CONTACT

I’m Dylan Moron, a France-born director and editor blending cinematic elegance with high energy visuals. Currently based in Frankfurt and working globally, I craft immersive visuals where refined cinematography meets fast-paced storytelling - moving seamlessly between commercials, brand content and short documentaries.

With over 8 years of experience working across 10+ countries, I’ve partnered with production companies and collaborated with global brands such as Puma, Adidas, Salomon, and Stevens Bikes.

Whether I’m on-location capturing moving images or diving deep into the edit, sound and color to bring the vision to life, my focus remains the same: pushing the visual edge to deliver high-impact imagery for brands that want to move as fast as they look.

Available in Frankfurt, Paris and worldwide.

If you’d like to work together, please send a message via the form below or contact me directly at contact@daynmoron.com
